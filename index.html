General Instructions

  1. The total duration of the examination is shown above. The exam consists of multiple sections, each with its own dedicated time.
  2. The clock at the top right of the screen will display the time remaining for the current section. When the section timer reaches zero, that section will be auto-submitted and locked.
  3. You cannot return to a section once its time has elapsed or you have moved to the next section.
  4. The Question Palette on the right side shows the status of each question using the following symbols:
1 Not Visited
2 Not Answered
3 Answered
4 Marked for Review
5 Answered & Marked for Review

Navigating & Answering a Question

  1. To select an option, click on the radio button next to the option.
  2. Click Save & Next to save your answer and move to the next question.
  3. Click Mark for Review & Next to mark a question for review.
  4. Click Clear Response to remove the selected answer.
  5. You can directly jump to any question within the current section by clicking on its number in the palette.
